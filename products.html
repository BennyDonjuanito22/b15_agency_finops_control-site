Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our plans and billing.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, and we prorate upgrades.

Is there a setup fee?

No. All plans start with a 14-day free trial with full feature access. After the trial, you'll be billed monthly. No setup fees, no hidden charges.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes — save 20% when you pay annually. Solo is $374/yr, Agency is $950/yr, and Enterprise is $2,390/yr. Contact us for custom enterprise agreements.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation, giving you time to export everything. After 90 days, all data is permanently and securely deleted.
