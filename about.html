Our Story

Built by operators, for operators

FinOpsControl was born from the frustration of managing finances across spreadsheets, bank portals, and accounting tools that weren't built for service businesses. We set out to build the financial command center we always wanted.

Our Mission

Every agency founder, freelancer, and service business owner deserves real-time visibility into their financial health — without needing a CFO on payroll or an accounting degree. FinOpsControl exists to democratize financial operations.

We believe that when business owners can see exactly where their money is going, how much each client actually costs, and what their cash position will look like next month, they make better decisions. Better decisions mean healthier businesses, more jobs, and more value delivered to the world.

Our Values

Transparency First

We build tools that surface truth — no hidden fees, no vanity metrics, no misleading dashboards. If the numbers are bad, you'll know immediately.

Security is Non-Negotiable

Financial data is sacred. We encrypt everything at rest and in transit, pursue SOC 2 compliance, and never sell or share your data with third parties.

Operator Empathy

Our team has run agencies and freelance businesses. We build the features we need ourselves, and we prioritize the workflows that matter most to operators.

Our Journey

From frustration to a platform trusted by thousands.

2022 — The Spark

James and Aisha meet at a fintech meetup. They discover they're both building internal dashboards to solve the same problem. They decide to build one product and open it to the world.

2023 — Beta Launch

FinOpsControl launches in private beta with 120 agencies. The core P&L and cash flow modules ship first. User feedback drives rapid iteration on the contractor tracking feature.

2024 — Public Launch

Public launch with three pricing tiers. Bank feed integration goes live, supporting 12,000+ financial institutions. Team grows to 20 people across engineering, product, and support.

2025 — Enterprise & API

Enterprise plan launches with multi-entity support and full API access. SOC 2 Type I audit completed. Platform surpasses $1B in tracked transactions.

2026 — Scale

AI-powered expense categorization and cash flow forecasting ship. 4,200+ businesses trust FinOpsControl for their daily financial operations. SOC 2 Type II in progress.
