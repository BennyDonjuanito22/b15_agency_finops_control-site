Pricing FAQ

Can I try before I buy?

Absolutely. Every plan includes a 14-day free trial with full feature access. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) as well as ACH bank transfers for annual plans.

Are there any transaction fees?

No. FinOpsControl charges a flat monthly or annual subscription. There are no per-transaction fees, no hidden charges, and no overage costs.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes. Registered nonprofits receive a 25% discount on any plan. Contact us with your 501(c)(3) documentation to get started.
